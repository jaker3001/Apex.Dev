## Feature Ideas

### Xactimate Integration
- [ ] **Xactimate PDF Parser** - Extract line items, totals, and categories from estimate PDFs
  - Why it matters: Faster estimate review, easier comparison to actual costs
  - Rough complexity: Large (PDF parsing, data extraction, UI)

- [ ] **Line Item Justification Generator** - AI writes IICRC-compliant justifications
  - Why it matters: Saves hours on adjuster negotiations
  - Rough complexity: Medium (prompt engineering, template system)

### Communication Automation
- [ ] **Adjuster Email Drafts** - AI drafts professional, firm responses to adjusters
  - Why it matters: Consistent tone, faster responses, better outcomes
  - Rough complexity: Small-Medium

- [ ] **Customer Update Templates** - Standard templates for common updates
  - Why it matters: Professional communication, saves time
  - Rough complexity: Small

### Field Operations
- [ ] **Equipment Tracking** - Track dehumidifiers, air movers, moisture meters
  - Why it matters: Know what's deployed where, maintenance scheduling
  - Rough complexity: Medium

- [ ] **Photo Documentation** - Organized job photos with AI tagging
  - Why it matters: Easier documentation, better claims support
  - Rough complexity: Medium-Large

### Business Intelligence
- [ ] **Job Profitability Calculator** - Compare estimate to actual labor/materials
  - Why it matters: Know which job types are most profitable
  - Rough complexity: Medium

- [ ] **Subcontractor Cost Analysis** - Track sub costs vs. in-house potential
  - Why it matters: Identify where hiring makes sense
  - Rough complexity: Medium
